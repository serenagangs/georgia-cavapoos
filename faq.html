FAQ

Helpful answers for families considering a Georgia Cavapoo.

Do you have puppies available right now?

Not at this time. Families may apply for upcoming litters.

How do I join the waitlist?

Start by completing our puppy application. We will review it and follow up with next steps.

When can puppies go home?

Puppies go home when they are developmentally ready and have received appropriate veterinary care.

Are puppies raised in your home?

Yes. Our puppies are raised in a home environment and introduced to daily household life.

Do you provide support after go-home day?

Yes. We want families to feel supported before and after bringing their puppy home.
