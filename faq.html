FAQ

Answers to common questions from families considering a Georgia Cavapoo.

Do you have puppies available right now?

Not at this time. Families may apply for upcoming litters.

How do I join the waitlist?

Start by completing the puppy application. We'll review it and follow up with next steps.

When can puppies go home?

Puppies go home when they are developmentally ready and have received appropriate veterinary care.

Are your puppies raised in your home?

Yes. Our puppies are raised in a home environment with daily care, handling, and socialization.

What comes with a puppy?

This section can include your go-home items, vet records, food, microchip, health guarantee, and support details.
