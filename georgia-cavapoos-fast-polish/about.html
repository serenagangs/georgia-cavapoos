About Georgia Cavapoos

Personal, thoughtful, and focused on family companions.

Georgia Cavapoos is centered around raising puppies with care, attention, and a strong foundation before they join their forever families.

Our story

Why we do this.

We love Cavapoos for their affectionate personalities, sweet expressions, and family-friendly temperaments. Our goal is not to be the biggest program — it is to be intentional with the puppies we raise and the families we work with.

We want prospective families to feel comfortable, informed, and confident from the first message through go-home day and beyond.
