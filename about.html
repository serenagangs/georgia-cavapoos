About Us

Our Program

Georgia Cavapoos is a home-based Cavapoo program in Georgia focused on raising healthy, affectionate companions for loving families.

Welcome in.

We believe choosing a puppy should feel personal, comfortable, and trustworthy. Our puppies are raised in our home, handled daily, and cared for with intention from the very beginning.

Our goal is simple: raise Cavapoos with sweet temperaments, healthy beginnings, and the confidence to become wonderful family companions.
