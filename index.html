Welcome to Georgia Cavapoos

We’re so glad you’re here. Choosing a puppy is an important decision, and we’re honored you’re considering our program.

Our goal is to raise Cavapoos who are beautiful, affectionate, and ready to become deeply loved companions. Each puppy is cared for in a home environment with daily attention, gentle socialization, and a focus on health and temperament from the very beginning.

Why families choose Georgia Cavapoos

A clear, careful process helps families feel comfortable from the first message to go-home day.

Raised in our home

Puppies experience everyday sounds, routines, handling, and affection from the start.

Health first

We prioritize responsible pairings, veterinary care, and parent dogs selected with intention.

Temperament focused

Our goal is a confident, affectionate companion who settles beautifully into family life.

Clear communication

Families receive honest updates and guidance throughout the process.

Continued support

We want our puppy families to feel supported long after their puppy goes home.

How do I join the waitlist?

Do you have puppies available right now?

Not at this time. We are currently accepting applications for upcoming litters.

When can puppies go home?

Most puppies go home around 8 weeks of age, depending on their development and veterinary guidance.
